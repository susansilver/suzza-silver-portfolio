About Suzza

Yo, this is a writer’s portfolio. I’m not here to give you my work history. If you want that, you will have to check me out on LinkedIn. Instead, I’ll tell you who I am and what this is all about.

I just turned 40 a year ago. As I enter this decade of my life, my mind is on my legacy. I want to leave behind some awesome stories. I take a lot of pride in my writing. My main focus is on the natural sciences and mathematics. I spend a lot of time on Twitter talking about #scicomm and #maths.

I’m still taking clients for my content writing business. Essentially, I’ll run your blog for a price. I have a decade of experience. I have worked in a variety of fields. My knowledge includes STEM, front-end development, WordPress, social media, community, and writing/blogging.
